Our Mission

Eventure Travels is the perfect app for your perfect travels. Here at Eventure Travels our goal is to make you happy. We aspire to be the best application to make scheduling your trip easier. Through our web application, users are able to plan their trip, whether it be a month long vacation, to a five hour stay. With our calendar, users will be able to see events which take place at their chosen destination at the time they will be there. Our application provides users with both a calendar and a map, to be able to see the things that they could possibly be encountering in their upcoming stay. .
